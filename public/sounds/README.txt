Vocara – Gimmick Sounds
======================

Place 2-3 second royalty-free .mp3 files here.

Required files:
  gimmick-hamburg.mp3   – Harbor/Elbe atmosphere (waves, seagulls, ship horn)
  gimmick-nairobi.mp3   – Savanna sunrise (birds, nature ambience)
  gimmick-welt.mp3      – Aurora/cosmic tones (ethereal chime or synth)

Sources for royalty-free sounds:
  https://freesound.org   (CC0 / CC-BY license, attribution in credits)
  https://pixabay.com/sound-effects/
  https://soundbible.com

If a file is missing or fails to load, no sound plays and no error is shown.
The animated overlay still appears regardless of sound availability.
